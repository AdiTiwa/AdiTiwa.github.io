Splack

Node JS

A new, custom made, language created to emulate the concepts behind React and other large javascript frameworks. In its final version it will come with a web server function, combined with a component system, as well as control-flow in files. It will have its own version of a style sheet, and will compile to html in runtime as to run the page as quick as possible.
E = MC2 Science Journal

React JS with Tailwind CSS

This website was created to replace the old version of the E = MC2 science journal website for the UChicago professor David Mazziotti. The science journal is a collection of many different Regeneron scholars' papers, and is meant to encourage the youth to work towards new projects in science and be inspired by the ideas shown by the papers on the website.
Stocky

Made with Flask(python) Back-End with HTML, CSS, and JS Front-End

This project was a submission to the IMSAHacks hackathon, under the theme economics and education. The purpose of this app was to teach people the intuition that comes with stock trading without utilizing buying power, and teaching people the strategies that come in stock trading with no penalties for mistakes. However, instead of simple paper trading sites, it helps you out along side you trying your hand at the strategies, with a robot named Stocky.
ClassCore

Made with Flask(python) Back-End with HTML, CSS, and JS Front-End

This project was a submission to a hackathon under the theme of education. It allows users to make documents and classes, all put together to make a learning platform made for specifically communities and researchers to teach the world about your specialties and your knowledge to teach others how you learn.
